Where We Are

GREATER CINCINNATI COMPUTER is conveniently located in Historic Silverton. Just 1.5 miles south of Kenwood town center off Montgomery Road on Plainfield Road.

Textual Assistance

From the north (Montgomery, Mason, etc.): Take I-71 south to the Montgomery Road exit in Kenwood. Turn right on Montgomery road and follow approximately 1.5 miles to Plainfield Road (Power 3 on the corner). Turn right on Plainfield road and we are located 4 blocks down on the right.
From the south (Downtown, Hyde Park, etc.): Take I-71 north to the Stewart road exit. Turn right off the exit and follow up to Montgomery road. Turn left on Montgomery road and follow to Plainfield Road (Power 3 on the corner). Turn right on Plainfield road and we are located 4 blocks down on the right.
