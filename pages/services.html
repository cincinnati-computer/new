On Site Service

Almost all our services offered in store are extended to our on site customers. We understand that there are times and situations which prohibit the down time of your system or network. We do everything within our power to get you back up and running in a seamless operation.

Repairs And Upgrades

Done in the Greater Cincinnati Computers store, on your site, in your home, even at your place of work. Anything from a simple memory upgrade to a full network install, no job is too big or too small.

Networking
